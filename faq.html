FAQ's

Dress Code/Weather

What should I wear?

We ask that everyone wear all black! We don’t have a black-tie formal requirement, but if you want to dress up feel free to do so! Otherwise, please come in what makes you comfortable. Our only request is the color - black!

What is the weather going to be like?

The weather should be in the 70-80’s when the sun is shining, and then down into the 50-60's when the sun goes down.

Will the wedding/reception be indoors or outdoors?

The wedding and reception will all be held at the same venue spot. There is a space of covered concrete for the cocktail hour and dancing at the reception, while the wedding ceremony and dinner will be held in the open air grassy area.
Ladies planning to wear heels should be mindful of walking through grass and dancing in their shoes all night!

RSVP Questions

Got questions about the RSVP process?

What date should I RSVP by?

Please RSVP by May 31st, 2025 to be included on the guest list!

Can I RSVP online or do I have to mail in an RSVP card?

Yes! Use the RSVP page on this website to RSVP for the festivities! You can also mail in your RSVP card through the mail, but please be sure to send it to arrive before the RSVP date.

Can I call to RSVP?

No. Calls can get missed or forgotten, please use the RSVP page on this website, or mail in your RSVP card by May 31st, 2025.

Wedding Weekend Questions

Where is the wedding/reception?

Both the wedding ceremony and the following reception will both be held at the Rosario’s Venue at the Bernardo Winery in Rancho Bernardo, CA.

The ceremony will begin at 4:00pm, we ask that guests arrive by 3:45pm at the latest to find your seats. If you happen to arrive after 4pm you will have to stand near the reception area until the coordinator can usher you in quietly.

Plus One's/Children

Am I allowed to bring a plus one?

We are having a smaller event, so we ask that only the people actually listed on the RSVP cards join us for the festivities.

 
Can I bring my kids?

There is only one tiny human being invited to this wedding, if any new ones happen to pop up before the November 1, 2025 date, please just reach out to Emelia or Nicholas to let them know. Otherwise, we ask that only those whose names are listed on the RSVP join us for the weekend.

What's on the Menu?

What type of food will be served at the reception?

There will be a cocktail hour after the wedding ceremony with a gluten and dairy free grazing table.
For dinner there will be a buffet with a salad, vegetable, pasta, and protein. Anything with gluten or dairy will be marked as such, but feel free to remind the servers of your own dietary restrictions as you go through the line.

 
Is there an open bar?

There is a bar that will serve a few different wines from the Bernardo Winery itself, along with sodas, and a water cooler.

Our Story

 

Thanks to the wonders of modern technology, we have Tinder to thank for bringing us together for a wedding November 1, 2025 (complaints can be directed to the app’s owner—not Emelia).
On Valentine's Day 2019, Emelia swiped right on a guy in a brown leather jacket with striking blue eyes, with what she thought was brown hair poking out from beneath a beanie. Little did she know, Nicholas, the blue-eyed blonde, had already swiped right on a few senior photos of her, taken in the beautiful wilds of Montana.

"A country girl," he thought

Despite the mix-ups, both were pleasantly surprised after getting to know each other, and by March 18th, they were officially a couple.

Fast-forward to February 2020: Emelia moved in with Nicholas to their first apartment. Two weeks later, the COVID-19 lockdown began, and they decided to adopt a cat - Luna - officially expanding their little family. By November, the trio had outgrown their one-bedroom apartment and moved into a two-bedroom in the same complex, where they’ve been making their home ever since.

On March 17th, 2023, they were driving to Los Angeles to celebrate their four-year anniversary. In classic Emelia fashion, a migraine seemed to throw a wrench in their plans. Nicholas, always understanding, suggested they stop at the Oceanside Pier for some fresh air. After a short walk to the end of the pier, they sat on an empty bench…

It was there Nicholas said the words that changed everything:

“I love you so much, and I want to love you forever. Will you be my wife?”

And now, here we are, ready to start the next chapter of our story - forever.
